What's New


  • Precautionary Steps - COVID-19

    Podar Education Network COVID-19 update -
    The COVID-19 crisis is the latest global challenge and we need to work together to slow the spread.
    As a Network of Schools spread across India, we take National and Global Health very seriously.
    As per the Government Guidelines, all Podar International Schools and Podar Jumbo Kids centers Pan India have been closed until further notice.
    All our Schools are also taking steps to protect the health and wellbeing of our community in line with Government Guidelines and World Health Organization (WHO) recommendations.
    Thank you for your patience in such difficult circumstances, and please know you are in our hearts and minds as we navigate our way.
